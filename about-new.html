About RPM Micro Solutions

Our Mission

RPM Micro Solutions specializes in precision micro-molding for the medical device industry. We partner with medical device OEMs to bring complex micro-components from concept to production with unmatched precision and quality.

Our integrated approach combines advanced materials expertise, precision tooling, micro-molding, and assembly services under one roof, enabling faster development cycles and superior quality outcomes for our clients.

Core Capabilities

  • Micro-injection molding (.004" wall thickness)
  • Precision hole drilling (Ø.002" diameter)
  • Complex geometries with R.001" edge radius
  • ISO 13485 certified quality systems
  • FDA compliant manufacturing processes
  • Material selection and validation
  • Prototype to production scalability

Industries We Serve

We focus exclusively on the medical device industry, serving companies developing:

Minimally Invasive Devices: Catheters, guidewires, and delivery systems requiring precise micro-components

Diagnostic Equipment: Microfluidic devices and precision components for diagnostic instruments

Therapeutic Devices: Drug delivery systems and implantable device components

Quality & Compliance

Quality is at the core of everything we do. Our facility operates under ISO 13485 certification with FDA-compliant manufacturing processes. We maintain comprehensive documentation and traceability systems to support regulatory submissions and ensure consistent, high-quality production.

Our team brings decades of experience in medical device manufacturing, with deep expertise in regulatory requirements, biocompatible materials, and validation processes specific to the medical industry.
